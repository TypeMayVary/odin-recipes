Mac and Cheese bites

Description

Mac and cheese bites are a delicious and cheesy snack made from macaroni pasta mixed with cheese, then breaded and fried or baked until golden brown.

Ingredients

  • 460g cooked macaroni pasta
  • 220g shredded cheddar cheese
  • 100g breadcrumbs
  • 65g grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 200Â°C.
  2. In a bowl, mix together the cooked macaroni, cheddar cheese, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, beaten egg, salt, and pepper.
  3. Scoop tablespoon-sized portions of the mixture and shape them into balls.
  4. Place the balls on a baking tray lined with parchment paper.
  5. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until golden brown and crispy.
  6. Let cool slightly before serving.
