Parmesan Cheese Crisps

Description

Parmesan cheese crisps are a delicious and easy-to-make snack. They are made by baking grated Parmesan cheese until it becomes crispy and golden brown.

Ingredients

  • 280g grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 200Â°C.
  2. Line a baking tray with parchment paper.
  3. In a bowl, mix together the grated Parmesan cheese, garlic powder, paprika, and black pepper.
  4. Scoop tablespoon-sized portions of the cheese mixture onto the prepared baking tray, leaving space between each scoop.
  5. Flatten each scoop slightly with the back of a spoon.
  6. Bake for 5-7 minutes or until the crisps are golden brown and crispy.
  7. Leave to cool before serving.
